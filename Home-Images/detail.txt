Description that can be added :There is an inherent relationship between Fashion and Interior design. Both the disciplines shelter the body, react to spatial constraints, rely on a creative process, and transform an idea from a two- dimensional concept into a three-dimensional reality. In this apartment, fashion heavily informed our design intent for the interior spaces; each room in the home was correlated to a specific fashion trend, culminating in a design that works for every season.
