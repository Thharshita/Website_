About Shiv Shakti Interior

Your Trusted Interior Design & Turnkey Partner

With years of expertise in interior design and contracting, Shiv Shakti Interior has established itself as a leading name across India for comprehensive design and execution solutions. We specialize in complete 2D design visualization and turnkey contracting, delivering end-to-end services from initial concept and design to final execution.

What We Do?

We design and transform spaces through innovative 2D visualization and meticulous craftsmanship across multiple disciplines:

2D Design & Visualization

Complete 2D design services including floor plans, elevations, and detailed layouts. We bring your vision to life through precise technical drawings and aesthetic visualizations before execution begins.

Residential Interiors

From luxury apartments to sprawling homes, we design and create personalized living spaces that reflect your lifestyle with complete home makeovers, modular kitchens, and custom wardrobes.

Commercial & Hospitality

We design and execute corporate offices, retail showrooms, restaurants, and hotels that enhance productivity, customer experience, and brand identity.

Complete Turnkey Solutions

End-to-end services from design to execution including painting, civil work, AC installation, electrical, carpentry, POP work, false ceilings, polishing, and tiling with seamless delivery.

Rustomjee Seasons - Luxury Home Interior

Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra

There is an inherent relationship between Fashion and Interior design. Both disciplines shelter the body, react to spatial constraints, rely on a creative process, and transform an idea from a two-dimensional concept into a three-dimensional reality. In this apartment, fashion heavily informed our design intent for the interior spaces; each room in the home was correlated to a specific fashion trend, culminating in a design that works for every season.

Sky City by Oberoi Realty - Multigenerational Living

Borivali East, Mumbai, Maharashtra

From the very first step inside, the home unfolds as a welcome retreat. Formed by combining two three-bedroom units into a sprawling 2,100-square-foot expanse, it celebrates the nuances of multigenerational living. The pièce de resistance is the open-plan living room, which unites the living, dining and kitchen area in a single, fluid space. We approached the project with a multi-focal design philosophy that harmonizes functionality with aesthetic elegance.
